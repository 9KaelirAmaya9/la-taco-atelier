## What Happens When You Switch?

### The Process:
1. **Your code stays on GitHub** (doesn't move)
2. **Vercel connects to your GitHub** repo
3. **Vercel builds and deploys** your code
4. **You get a URL** (like: your-site.vercel.app)
5. **Every time you push to GitHub**, Vercel auto-deploys

### What Stays the Same:
- ‚úÖ All your code
- ‚úÖ All your styles

- ‚úÖ All your functionality
